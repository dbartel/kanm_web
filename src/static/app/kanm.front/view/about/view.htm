What is KANM?

The college station of College Station

KANM is the student radio station at Texas A&M University

We Play Underground Music

DJs program shows playing music encompassing nearly every genre possible - anything from indie pop to hip-hop to punk to jazz and so on.

Our focus is on providing an outlet for music typically ignored by commercial radio. We encourage DJ's to branch out and try music they haven't listened to before.

100% Student Run
